FAQs

How often should I take my pet to the vet?

It is recommended to take your pet to the vet at least once a year for a general check-up. Puppies, kittens, and senior pets may require more frequent visits.

What should I feed my new puppy or kitten?

Young pets need food specially formulated for their growth. Look for high-quality, age-appropriate pet food, and follow the feeding guidelines provided by your vet.

How do I introduce my pet to a new home?

Start by confining your pet to one room and gradually introduce them to the rest of the house. Provide familiar items like toys or blankets to help them feel secure.
